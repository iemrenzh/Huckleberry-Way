Common Questions

Everything you need to know

What level of support do you provide?

We offer a minimum 1:2 support ratio, with higher levels including 1:1 available based on individual needs assessments conducted during intake.

Are your camps accessible for wheelchair users?

Yes. We partner with accredited campsites that have fully accessible facilities, and all activities are designed to be inclusive across a range of ability levels.

How is NDIS funding used for camps?

NDIS funding typically covers support worker costs. Depending on your plan, it may also contribute to transport and activity fees. Our intake team will walk you through this.

What should I pack for camp?

Once your booking is confirmed we send a detailed packing list tailored to the specific camp location, climate, and activities planned.

What are the sleeping arrangements?

Arrangements vary by campsite and can include accessible cabins or modern spacious tents. Preferences are discussed and confirmed during the intake process.

Can you cater to special dietary needs?

Absolutely. Our camp chefs cater to all dietary requirements, allergies, and preferences — confirmed and planned before each camp begins.

Our Story

Creating adventures that change lives

Huckleberry Way was founded on a simple belief: everyone deserves the freedom of the outdoors. Since 2020, we've supported hundreds of Australians with disability to explore, connect, and grow through the power of nature.

We work with NDIS participants, families, and support coordinators to design experiences that are genuinely safe, genuinely inclusive — and genuinely fun.

Our Mission

To create accessible and empowering outdoor adventures for NDIS participants, fostering confidence, connection, and freedom in nature.

Our Vision

To be Australia's leading provider of inclusive outdoor experiences — making the bush, beach and wilderness available to everyone.
